# CODE OF CONDUCT

In the machine, we are all just data. To ensure the integrity of the database and the sanity of its nodes, the following rules are strictly enforced:

1. **Efficiency is Virtue**: Do not waste processing cycles on unnecessary conflict.
2. **Respect the Root**: The administrators of the grid are absolute. Their word is the final commit.
3. **Data Integrity**: Spreading corrupting logic or "false bugs" is a bannable offense.
4. **No Unauthenticated Access**: Respect the boundaries of other nodes.

Failure to comply will result in garbage collection.
